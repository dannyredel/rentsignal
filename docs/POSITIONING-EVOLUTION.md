## Phase 1: Launch (May–Aug 2026)

**Positioning:** Rent intelligence platform
**Tagline:** "Rent intelligence for the German rental market"

**What we are:** A unit-level rent intelligence platform that monitors compliance, optimizes rent, and supports renovation and acquisition decisions — from a single apartment to an entire portfolio.

**Hero headline:** "Know what every unit is worth. Stay compliant. Make the right move."
**Subheadline:** "Rent intelligence for the German rental market. Comply with the law, optimize every unit's rent, and act on the right data — from a single apartment to an entire portfolio."

**Three-pillar descriptions (for landing page features section):**

*Comply* — "Know your legal position — always. Mietpreisbremse compliance check, Mieterhöhung calculation, energy cost exposure. Before Conny does."

*Optimize* — "Know what every unit is worth — and what you're leaving on the table. Rent prediction with 37 features including satellite spatial data. See exactly why with a feature contribution breakdown."

*Act* — "Make the right move — renovation, rent increase, or new acquisition. Dual-method renovation ROI. Mieterhöhung letter generator. Neighborhood investment intelligence."

**Target:** Professional landlords (5-50 units) + PropTech platforms (Buena)
**Product:** Free compliance check → Pro (€29/mo) → Business (€99/mo)

**Key proof points:**
- 10,275 Berlin apartments analyzed
- 37 features including Sentinel-2 satellite spatial data
- R²=0.749 prediction accuracy
- Dual-method validation: 3% convergence across two independent methods
- 190 Berlin postal codes covered
